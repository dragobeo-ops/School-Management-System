World Class library

A school library is an essential place where students can explore a wide variety of books, gain new knowledge, and strengthen their reading skills. It provides a quiet and comfortable environment that helps students focus on studying, completing assignments, and doing research. With storybooks, reference materials, and digital resources, the library encourages curiosity and supports academic growth. It also helps students develop good reading habits and a love for learning, making it an important part of every school.

Largest Play Ground

Our basketball playground is one of the most lively and enjoyable areas of our school. It has a smooth, well-marked court with strong hoops, which makes playing more fun and safe. Students gather there to practice dribbling, passing, and shooting, while also improving their teamwork and confidence. During sports periods and after school, the playground becomes a place full of energy, where friendly matches and small competitions take place

Tasty ad Healthy Food

Our school cafeteria serves tasty and healthy food that keeps students energized throughout the day. It offers fresh meals made with clean ingredients, including fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and nutritious snacks. The food is prepared in a hygienic kitchen, ensuring both safety and good flavor. Students enjoy a variety of options that are delicious yet wholesome, helping them stay active, focused, and ready to learn. The cafeteria truly supports our well-being by providing meals that are both satisfying and good for our health.
